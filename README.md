# TheYouIWatched
A FYP game.

Game Overview
Genre: Interactive movie game / FMV
Target Platform: PC
Player Count: 1
Session Length: at least 60 minutes 

Player Experience
Target Audience: Explorers 
Emotional Goals: Anxiety, worry, boredom
Motivation Design: 
Intrinsic: Unlocking the keyword, find the right keywords & watch the right videos
Extrinsic: Allowing players to explore, make choices, and find secrets fuels curiosity.

Game Concept
To make an interactive movie experience about different death stories that relate to the real case studies. The player can explore the character's thoughts, as well as their story, through the live streamers' clips, video by video

Design Goals
Reinterpreting these true stories aims to prompt reflection on why they happened

High Concept Statement
You are a detective who receives an email requesting information about three deceased livestreamers. During the investigation, you will discover your own past, and "you" may choose to oppose you.

Message
Humans are two-faced, even multi-faceted. People are more fragile than we imagine, yet stronger too, or both at once. A single word can sway their will to live, yet never erase their resolve. In today’s rampant virtual world, we project ourselves online, where every action and emotion gets amplified. We love being spectators, but when facing the same situation yourself, what choice would you make? Aren’t you just like those you once “controlled”?
